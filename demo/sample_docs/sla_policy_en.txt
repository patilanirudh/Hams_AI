HamsAI Support SLA Policy
==========================

1. Overview
This document defines the Service Level Agreement (SLA) for all HamsAI technical support tiers. All response and resolution times are measured in business hours (Sunday–Thursday, 09:00–18:00 AST) unless a 24/7 plan is purchased.

2. Support Tiers

2.1 Standard Support (included with all plans)
- First Response SLA: 24 business hours
- Resolution SLA: 5 business days
- Channels: Email only
- Price: Included

2.2 Gold Support
- First Response SLA: 8 business hours
- Resolution SLA: 2 business days
- Channels: Email + phone
- Price: 2,500 SAR/month

2.3 Premium Support
- First Response SLA: 1 hour (Severity 1) / 4 hours (Severity 2) / 8 hours (Severity 3)
- Resolution SLA: 4 hours (Sev1) / 8 hours (Sev2) / 2 business days (Sev3)
- Channels: Email + phone + dedicated Slack channel
- Price: 7,500 SAR/month
- 24/7 coverage for Severity 1 incidents

2.4 Enterprise Support
- First Response SLA: 15 minutes (Severity 1) / 2 hours (Severity 2)
- Resolution SLA: 2 hours (Sev1) / 4 hours (Sev2)
- Channels: All channels + dedicated named account manager
- Price: Custom (starting at 25,000 SAR/month)
- 24/7/365 coverage

3. Severity Definitions

Severity 1 (Critical): Complete system outage or data loss affecting all users. Production environment down.
Severity 2 (High): Major feature unavailable; significant impact on business operations. Workaround not available.
Severity 3 (Medium): Feature degraded; partial impact. Workaround available.
Severity 4 (Low): Minor issue; cosmetic or documentation requests.

4. SLA Credits

If HamsAI fails to meet the agreed SLA, customers are eligible for service credits:
- Miss by up to 2x the SLA: 5% credit on monthly invoice
- Miss by 2x–5x: 10% credit
- Miss by more than 5x: 20% credit
Maximum credits per month: 30% of monthly subscription fee.

5. Exclusions
SLA does not apply during scheduled maintenance windows (announced 48 hours in advance), force majeure events, or issues caused by customer-side infrastructure or third-party services.

Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Version: 2.3
